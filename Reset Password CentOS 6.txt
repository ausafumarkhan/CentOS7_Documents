                       ##### ROOT/USER PASSWORD RESET IN CENTOS 6 #####################

step 1: Restart the machine
step 2: Click on splash screen and press 'a' 
step 3: Go to the end of line and type "1" or "single" at the end
step 4: a shell appears
step 5: Type "passwd" and give new password twice     
---Note: You can change password any user here with command: passwd <user_name> 
step 6: Type "init 5" to go back to GUI mode
